Curry Chicken with Coconut and Ananas

This is a recipe of my dear friend.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast 600 g
  • Snow peas 250 g
  • Fresh chili peppers 3
  • Spring onions 3
  • Ginger 1 piece
  • Parsley half a bundle
  • Ananas (Pineapple) 200 g
  • Garlic gloves 2
  • Sunflower oil 5 tablespoons
  • Lemon 1 fruit
  • Cumin half a tablespoon
  • Coconut milk 400 g

Steps

  1. Wash the snow peas and cut them in about 2 cm long pieces.
  2. Cut the ananas and chicken breast also in pieces of about 2 cm.
  3. Cook the snow peas for about 2 to 5 minutes until they are firm to the bite. Then drain them.
  4. Wash the chili peppers and remove the kernels, if you want it less spicy. Then chop them roughly.
  5. Wash the spring onions and parsley, peel the garlic and ginger and chop them too.
  6. Grate the lemon zest.
  7. Pur√©e the chili peppers, spring onions, parsley, garlic, ginger and lemon zest together with 2 tablespoons of the sunflower oil.
  8. Heat up 3 tablespoons of sunflower oil in a wok or pot.
  9. Sear the chicken breast in the wok for a few minutes then push them to the side.
  10. Add the chili paste mix in the middle and sear it for about 3 minutes.
  11. Add the snow peas and ananas and stir everything together.
  12. Stir in the coconut milk and let it simmer for about 5 minutes.
