Kaiserschmarrn

This is a recipe of my dear friend.

Ingredients

  • Eggs 5
  • Milk 125 ml
  • Flour 125 g
  • Salt 1 pinch
  • Raisins 3 tablespoons
  • Sugar 1 tablespoon
  • Butter 25 g
  • Powdered sugar according to taste
  • Compote or apple purée according to taste

Steps

  1. Seperate the egg whites from the yolks.
  2. Mix the flour, milk and sugar with a pinch of salt.
  3. Stirr in the yolks and raisins.
  4. Beat the egg whites until stiff.
  5. Gently fold the stiff egg whites into the yolk mixture to make the dough.
  6. Heat up the butter in a pan and fill the dough into the pan. Take care to not add too much heat or it will burn!
  7. After 1 to 2 minutes, cut the dough with a spatula in smaller pieces to turn them around and cook them until the underside has a nice colour.
  8. Now chop the pieces into smaller pieces.
  9. Serve it and add some powdered sugar on top. Tastes best with some compote or apple purée!
